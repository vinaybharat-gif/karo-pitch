About Karo Pitch

Empowering founders across Bharat to connect with investors and scale their dreams

What is Karo Pitch?

Karo Pitch is a curated platform that bridges the gap between promising startups and serious investors. We organize pitch events across India, giving founders a chance to showcase their businesses to top-tier investors looking for the next big thing.

Why We Created It

Thousands of founders are building amazing businesses but lack access to investors. We saw this gap and created Karo Pitch to democratize funding opportunities for entrepreneurs across Bharat, not just in metro cities.

Our Mission

To support founders across Bharat by connecting them with the right investors through curated pitch events and our discovery platform. We believe every great startup deserves a chance to shine.

How It Works

Simple 4-step process to transform your startup journey

1

Apply with Pitch Deck

Submit your startup details and pitch deck through our simple application process. Tell us about your vision, traction, and funding requirements.

2

Get Shortlisted

Our team reviews every application and shortlists the most promising startups based on innovation, market potential, and founder capability.

3

Pitch to Investors

Get selected startups pitch live to a panel of accredited investors. Make your case and answer questions in a professional environment.

4

Raise & Scale

Close your funding round and get access to our network of mentors, resources, and growth opportunities to scale your business.
